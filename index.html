Our Story

My story began when I wanted to gift something special for my niece's arrival… My first handmade bouquet was made with love, and my passion kept growing day by day.

I created designs for family moments, then for friends… Their support inspired me to launch Velvet Bloom.

Velvet Bloom means 'the velvet flower' — because satin flowers never fade, and the memory stays for years 🤍

Thank you to everyone who supported me, even with a kind word 🫶🏼
